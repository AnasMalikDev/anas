Version 1.90. Changelog:

> Source code updated to: v9.5.8.0. 
> Add Power Saving Mode: You can switch to Power Saving Mode or turn off autoplay, animations or effects separately in Settings > Power Saving.
> Added reading time in small groups: In groups of up to 100 participants, you can now see the time when messages have been read by each of the participants.
> Add accurate playback speed: Videos, podcasts, audio and video messages can now be played at any speed from 0.2x to 2.5x using the slider.
> Add an option: to hide the "Saved Messages" button from the list.
> Add an option: to hide the slash bot button in the chat.
> Bug fixes: and other minor improvements.
